Stranger Things

Stranger Things is a popular sci-fi horror series.

It is set in the 1980s in the fictional town of Hawkins, Indiana.

The show features a group of kids who encounter supernatural forces and secret government experiments while trying to find their missing friend.

The series has received critical acclaim for its writing, acting, and nostalgic references to 1980s pop culture.
